AI-Powered Healthcare Coordination

Your Medical History, Unified & Auditable

MediFlow aggregates fragmented medical records across providers into a single, trustworthy timeline. Understand your complete care journey with AI-backed summaries tied to source documents.

Core Capabilities

MediFlow brings clarity and continuity to fragmented healthcare.

Multimodal Document Parsing

Upload and extract insights from PDFs, discharge summaries, prescriptions, lab reports, and medical images—all automatically organized.

Chronological Timeline

Automatically synthesize consultations, medications, tests, and treatments into a clear, auditable sequence across all providers.

Medication Conflict Detection

AI flags duplicate prescriptions and dangerous interactions across your care network using semantic analysis—before they cause harm.

Care Gap Alerts

Never miss a follow-up or preventive care deadline. MediFlow proactively notifies you of appointments, screenings, and medication refills.

Complete Audit Trail

Every AI summary is linked to the original source text. Verify insights instantly and maintain full transparency with your providers.

Conversational Health Coach

Chat with an AI that remembers your scheduling preferences, medication constraints, and full medical context for personalized guidance.
