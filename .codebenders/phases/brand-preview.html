Your complete medical history, unified

MediFlow brings together fragmented patient records across providers. Get instant access to your full medical timeline, identify care gaps, and stay coordinated with your healthcare team.

How MediFlow Works

Intelligent coordination across your entire care network

Unified Records

Upload and parse medical documents from any provider—discharge summaries, prescriptions, test results, and imaging reports all in one place.

Smart Timeline

Automatically organize consultations, medications, tests, and treatments into a chronological view you can understand at a glance.

Proactive Alerts

Never miss a follow-up or preventive care milestone. Get notified about scheduling windows and medication interactions across your network.

Conflict Detection

AI-powered semantic analysis identifies duplicate or conflicting medications across your entire health network automatically.

Complete Audit Trail

Every AI-generated summary is bound to source text snippets. Full transparency and traceability for clinical confidence.

Plain Language

Clinical terminology translated into clear explanations in multiple languages. Healthcare that's accessible to everyone.
